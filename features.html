Risk Management Tools

PrimeXBT offers a range of risk management tools to help you protect your investments:

  • Stop-Loss Orders: Automatically close your position if the market moves against you by a specified amount.
  • Take-Profit Orders: Lock in profits by automatically closing your position when it reaches a certain level of profitability.
  • Trailing Stop Orders: Dynamically adjust your stop-loss as the market moves in your favor.
  • Guaranteed Stop-Loss Orders: Ensure your position is closed at the exact price you specify, even in volatile market conditions (additional fee applies).
